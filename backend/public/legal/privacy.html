Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Receipt. At Sydnical, we value your privacy. We intentionally designed our chronological journaling service to collect as little data as possible. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the essential information needed to operate your digital Ledger and connect you with your friends.

  • Account Data: We collect your email address, username, and password when creating an account.
  • User Logs: The photos, text snippets, emojis, location tags, and music metadata you supply when "printing" a receipt.
  • Device Identifiers: We store a push notification token provided by your operating system strictly to deliver notifications (e.g., when your Weekly Montage is ready).

3. How We Use Information

Our business model is not based on ads. Therefore, we do not sell your personal data.

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Receipt application.
  • Populate your private chronological feed and share it securely with the friends you have explicitly approved.
  • Generate your automated Weekly Montages.
  • Send you critical service-related communications and push notifications.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We only share data when required by law, or with critical infrastructure providers (like our cloud hosting and database providers) that are bound by strict confidentiality obligations to process data solely on our behalf.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You own your data. You may request an export of your full ledger at any time directly through the app. You also have the right to permanently delete your account at any time via the System -> Data & Privacy screen. When you issue a deletion request, all your historical logs, friendships, and metadata will be permanently expunged from our live servers via an automated cascading deletion process.

6. Security

We safeguard your data. All data travels over encrypted networks (TLS 1.3). User passwords are cryptographically hashed before they are stored in our databases. We restrict our own internal access to your private daily logs.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you within the App or by email prior to the change becoming effective.

8. Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at contact@sydnical.com.
